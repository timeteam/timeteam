***
<b>Simon Singh</b> 2005. _Fermat's Last Theorem_ London: Harper Collins. 340 pp.

***

Not just a popular account of the efforts of multiple mathematicians to solve Fermat's last theorem, but more so a splendid introduction to the rarified atmosphere of mathematicians working on number theory.
